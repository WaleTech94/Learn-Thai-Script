Transliteration key
Tone marks on vowels: a mid · à low · â falling · á high · ǎ rising. Doubled vowels (aa, ii, uu) are long. or = the vowel in "for" with no r sound. bp/dt are unaspirated p/t — between English b/p and d/t.
