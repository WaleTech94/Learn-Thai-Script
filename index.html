The class cheat code
Memorise two small sets — everything else is low class by default.

Mid (9): ก จ ฎ ฏ ด ต บ ป อ
Thai kids chant: ไก่จิกเด็กตายบนปากโอ่ง — "the chicken pecks the kid dead on the rim of the jar."

High (11): ข ฃ ฉ ฐ ถ ผ ฝ ศ ษ ส ห
All breathy or hissy sounds — kh, ch, th, ph, f, s, h.

Low: the other 24. Never memorise them.

The deep pattern: high/low pairs (ข/ค, ถ/ท, ผ/พ, ฝ/ฟ, ส/ซ, ห/ฮ) are the same sounds twice — so every sound can reach every tone.

Transliteration key
Tone marks on vowels: a mid · à low · â falling · á high · ǎ rising. Doubled vowels (aa, ii, uu) are long. or = the vowel in "for" with no r sound. bp/dt are unaspirated p/t — between English b/p and d/t.
